Tongue Twister

Believe you are good with numbers and have a pinch of luck…..

Then you are the perfect fit for this exciting game. The literary wing brings to you your college’s own lottery....The English Tambola… A game where you can win …not with any special skills but just luck..

A caller randomly selects numbers and calls them out for a group of players who each have a card of random numbers in a grid. The players mark the numbers off their cards as they are called.

So come and be part of this fun throbbing game that will provide lots of excitements and awesome moments...
