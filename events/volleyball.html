Volleyball

Rules

Boys
1.Concerning the gameplay, all standard FIVB rules will be followed and maximum of 12 players are allowed per team.
2."Rotation" rule will be followed during all the matches unless both the teams agree to play "fixed".
3.The format of the tournament will be decided upon the arrival... of teams.
4.Set will be of 25 points.
5.The schedule will be provided to the teams upon their arrival and is subject to change at any point of time, so the teams are requested to check the notice board regularly.
6.In case of any dispute during the match, the decision of the referees will be considered final and binding.
7.Any disciplinary violation by any member of the team will lead to disqualification of the team.
8.All the players will be required to show their ID cards before the beginning of the match failing which the defaulting individual will not be allowed to play.
9.If a team arrives late by more than 15 minutes, it will be awarded a 20(250, 250) loss.

Girls

1."Rotation" rule will be followed during all matches, unless both the teams agree to play "fixed".
2.Each match(singles/doubles) is best of 3 sets of 21 points.
3.All standard FIVB rules applicable.
4.The format of the tournament will be decided upon the arrival of teams.
5.All league matches will be best out of 3 sets. Each set will be of 25 points.
6.The schedule of the tournament will be provided to the teams upon their arrival and is subject to change at any point of time, so the teams are requested to check the notice board regularly.
7.In case of any dispute during the match, the decision of the referees will be considered final and binding.
8.Any disciplinary violation by any member of the team will lead to disqualification of that team.
9.All the players will be required to show their ID cards before the beginning of the match failing which the defaulting individual will not be allowed to play.
10.Teams should report to the court half an hour before their match.
11.If a team arrives late by more than 15 minutes, it will be awarded a 20 (250,250) loss.
12.Teams should bring their own balls for practice. Match balls will be provided for the matches.
