Cricket

Rules

1.Maximum 15 players are allowed in a team.
2.Tournament will be played on T20 format. Normal T20 rules apply.
3.Tournament will be played on knock out format.
4.Tie in the game will be resolved by a super over.
5.Teams should report 15 minutes before the scheduled time if the team is late by more than 5 minutes, the match would be forfeited to the opposition team.
6.All the teams should come in whites.
7.Team has to complete its quota of overs in 1hour 25 minutes.
8.If the 1st bowling team fails to complete then they will be penalized and have to chase the score in the number of overs they have bowled in the specified time.
9.If the 2nd bowling team fails to complete then runs equal to (current run rate of the batting team * the no of overs) left will be added to the batting side total,also they will have to bowl the overs left.
10.Decision of Umpires and Organizing committee shall be final and binding.
