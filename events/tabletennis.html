Table-Tennis

Rules

Boys 1.Format Of Play : singles(A vs X),singles(B vs Y), doubles and reverse singles(A vs Y & B vs X).
2.All games will be best of 5.
3.Players playing singles won't be allowed to repeat in doubles.
4.Maximum number of 5 and minimum number of 3 players are allowed in a team.
Girls 1.It is a team event requiring a minimum of 3 players and a maximum of 5 players.
2.All games will be best of 5 .
3.Description of the 5 sets is as follows: First two sets are singles matches,to be played by two different players A and B. Third set is a doubles match, to be played by either two new players (C and D) or one new player(C or D) and one of the single players(A or B). then reverse singles(A vs Y & B vs X).
