Badminton

Rules

Boys 1.Format of Play : Best of 5(in the order), Singles, Doubles, Singles.
2.Each match(singles/doubles) is best of 3 sets of 21 points.
3.Teams for every game are submitted before the match (specifying who will be playing 1st singles, 1st doubles and 2nd singles).This team can not be changed after submission.
4.Latest IBF rules will be followed.
5.Teams of maximum 5 players and minimum 3 players.
6.Only two players can play in two matches (one singles and one doubles).
Girls 1.Format of Play : Best of 3(in the order), Singles, Doubles, Singles.
2.Each match(singles/doubles) is best of 3 sets of 21 points.
3.Teams for every game are submitted before the match (specifying who will be playing 1st singles, 1st doubles and 2nd singles).This team can not be changed after submission.
4.Latest BWF rules will be followed.
5.Teams of maximum 5 players and minimum 3 players.
6.Only two players can play in two matches (one singles and one doubles).
